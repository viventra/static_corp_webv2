Solution Themes

Our product development focus

Viventra is a product development company. Our product strategy is built for the long-term around the concept of Solution themes.

Solution themes allow Viventra to choose its area of focus among the infinitude of opportunities in the customer problem space.

We use a disciplined approach to maintain a sharp focus and be effective by adapting to changing trends and new problems.

Viventra is actively working on 3 Solution themes: Ideation, Execution and Optimization.
